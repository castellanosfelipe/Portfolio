## Método / Method

- **Overline:** `The method`
- **H2:** `A simple cycle, repeated with discipline.`

| Paso | Título | Cuerpo | Output |
|---|---|---|---|
| 01 | `Listen` | `Real friction never shows up in the roadmap: it lives in user interviews, support tickets and pre-sales calls. That's where I start, before touching anything.` | `→ A friction map, prioritized by impact.` |
| 02 | `Measure` | `Instrument before you opine: activation, conversion, retention and cohorts. Data separates symptoms from causes; if there's no data, step one is getting it.` | `→ A clear baseline and the bottleneck, pinpointed.` |
| 03 | `Experiment` | `Every bet becomes a falsifiable hypothesis, with success criteria and sample size defined before writing a single line of code. No retroactive cheating.` | `→ An honest decision: scale, iterate or discard.` |
| 04 ↻ | `Compound` | `What wins gets folded into the product and the team's playbook; what loses leaves a documented lesson. The cycle starts again, one step higher.` | `→ A system that improves quarter after quarter.` |
